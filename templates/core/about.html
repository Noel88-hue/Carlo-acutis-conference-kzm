About the Kizhakkambalam Conference

Who We Are

A Youth Wing Rooted in Service

The Kizhakkambalam Conference is the youth wing of the Society of St. Vincent de Paul (SSVP), functioning under St. Antony's Forona Church in Kizhakkambalam, Kerala. We carry forward the Society's founding charism — Serviens in Spe, "We Serve in Hope" — through the hands and energy of the parish's young people.

Our patron is St. Carlo Acutis, the young Italian who became known as "God's Influencer" for living an ordinary teenage life — football, video games, friendships — while quietly anchoring it all in daily Mass and the Eucharist. We chose him as our namesake because his witness speaks directly to who we are: young people who don't see faith and ordinary life as separate things, but as one and the same.

Our Roots

The Society of St. Vincent de Paul was founded in 1833, inspired by St. Vincent de Paul's own 17th-century work among Paris's poor and sick. Conferences like ours exist worldwide, each one a small, local circle of volunteers committed to person-to-person charity — not just funding causes, but showing up.

Our Parish Home

St. Antony's Forona Church in Kizhakkambalam has long been a center of community life in the area. Our conference operates from within this parish, drawing its volunteers, its formation, and its sense of purpose from the same community that gathers here every week.

What We Do

Serve · Love · Transform

Following the motto on our own seal — Serve, Love, Transform — our work spans several areas of parish and community life:

Helping the Poor
Direct support to families and individuals facing hardship
Serving the Sick
Visits, comfort, and practical care for the unwell and elderly
Education & Mentoring
Tutoring and guidance for children in our community
Building Community
Gatherings and formation that keep our youth wing united in purpose

This website exists to keep a clear, transparent record of that work — every social program we run, every event we plan, every rupee raised, and the minutes from every meeting where we decide what comes next.
